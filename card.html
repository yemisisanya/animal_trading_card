Leafy Sea Dragon

They are known in Australia as a source of good luck.

  • Scientific Name: Phycodurus eques
  • Average Length: 8 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 5-10 years
  • Habitat:Sand patches in Water

They are called Leafy Sea Dragons because they have a leaf-like body. This allows them stay hidden from predators. The females are generally an inch or two smaller than the male sea dragons. Their structure makes them one of the most unique looking of all species in the world.
